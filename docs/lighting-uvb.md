# ☀️ UVB & Lighting: The Science of "Artificial Sun"

Turtles require high-quality UVB lighting to synthesize Vitamin D3, which is critical for calcium absorption and metabolic bone health.

## 🌡️ Why We Track Bulb Decay
UVB output decreases over time, even if the bulb still looks "bright" to the human eye.
- **T5 HO Linear Tubes**: Replace every 12 months.
- **Compact Fluorescent (CFL)**: Replace every 6 months.
- **Mercury Vapor Bulbs**: Replace every 12 months.

## 📊 Gold Standard Metrics
Using a Solarmeter 6.5 is the only way to accurately measure UVB output (UVI - UV Index).
- **Target UVI**: 1.0 - 3.0 (for most aquatic turtles in the basking zone).
- **Safe Distance**: Varies by bulb type; always refer to the manufacturer's distance chart.
