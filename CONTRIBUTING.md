# Contributing to Turbo's Mission 🐢

We love "The Turtle Clan" and welcome contributions from herpetologists, hobbyists, and developers!

## How to Help
1. **Data Submissions:** Update `data/nutrition-table.json` with safe foods or `data/vet-directory.csv` with trusted herp-vets.
2. **Care Guides:** Draft or edit Markdown files in `docs/` regarding filtration, lighting, or species-specific care.
3. **App Development:** Help build the "Shell-health" dashboard in `src/`.
4. **Infographics:** Submit high-quality diagrams to `assets/`.

## Standards
- **Science-First:** Always cite your sources (e.g., Reptifiles, herpetological studies).
- **Positive Vibes:** We are a community of learners. Be kind and helpful.

Thank you for helping us make the world a better place for turtles!
