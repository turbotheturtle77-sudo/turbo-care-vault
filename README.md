# 🐢 Turbo the Turtle (The Turtle Clan)

Welcome to the **Turbo Care Vault**, the official repository for **@turbo-the-turtle**. Our mission is to provide open-source, science-backed resources for reptile enthusiasts.

## 🌟 Mission
To empower "The Turtle Clan" with data-driven husbandry tools and knowledge to provide "Gold Standard" care.

## 🛠️ Project Structure
- **/apps/shell-sync-web**: The Next.js/Zustand "Shell-health" dashboard.
- **/docs**: Care guides, lighting science, and filtration setups.
- **/data**: Nutritional tables (Safe vs. Toxic) and herp-vet directories.
- **/scripts**: Calculation tools and IoT monitoring (ESP32/Arduino).
- **/assets**: Infographics and diagrams for the community.

## 🤝 Community
Check out our [Care Wiki](./docs/README.md) or contribute to our [Nutritional Table](./data/nutrition-table.json). See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) to help us build the ultimate reptile resource!
